Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl

Vegetarian
25
MINUTES
4
SERVINGS
385
CALORIES
4.8
RATING

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 large cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

1

In a medium saucepan, bring the vegetable broth to a boil. Add the rinsed quinoa, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes until the liquid is fully absorbed and quinoa is tender.

2

Remove from heat and fluff the quinoa with a fork. Let it cool for 5-10 minutes to room temperature.

3

In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooled quinoa, chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion. Toss gently to mix.

4

In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and black pepper to create the dressing.

5

Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss gently to coat all ingredients evenly.

6

Top with crumbled feta cheese, fresh parsley, and mint. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 3 days in an airtight container.
