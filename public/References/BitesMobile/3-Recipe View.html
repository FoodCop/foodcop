Avocado Toast Supreme

Prep Time

15 min

Calories

250 kcal

Servings

2

Vegetarian Quick Breakfast

Ingredients

Ripe avocados

2 pieces

Whole grain bread

4 slices

Fresh eggs

2 pieces

Fresh lemon juice

1 lemon

Salt and black pepper

To taste

Red pepper flakes

Optional

Extra virgin olive oil

1 tbsp

Cherry tomatoes

6 pieces, halved

Cooking Instructions

15 min
1

Toast the Bread

Toast the whole grain bread slices in a toaster or on a grill pan until golden brown and crispy on both sides. This should take about 3-4 minutes.

2

Prepare Avocado Mixture

Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash with a fork until your desired consistency. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper, then mix well.

3

Poach the Eggs

Bring a pot of water to a gentle simmer. Create a whirlpool in the water and carefully drop in the eggs. Cook for 3-4 minutes for runny yolks, or longer for firmer yolks.

4

Assemble the Toast

Spread the mashed avocado mixture generously on each slice of toasted bread. Top with cherry tomato halves and drizzle with olive oil.

5

Final Touch

Carefully place a poached egg on top of each avocado toast. Garnish with red pepper flakes if desired. Serve immediately while warm.

Pro Tip

For perfectly poached eggs, use the freshest eggs possible and add a splash of vinegar to the simmering water.
